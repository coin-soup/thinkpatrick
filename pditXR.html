Medical Training Simulation

C++ Unity HoloLens Android Haptics

01. Context

Medical students need to practice high-stakes surgical procedures where the sensation of resistance and the precision of the cut are vital. These simulations must run on devices like HoloLens, Android, and Haptics.

02. Challenge

How do we make repetitive medical drills engaging enough that students want to revisit them daily, rather than seeing it as a chore?

03. Solution

Creating medical training simulations using Unity and C++ allows you to combine the ease of rapid UI prototyping with the raw performance needed for complex anatomical physics & computer vision. For medical students, these simulations must bridge the gap between "gaming" and "clinical practice" to ensure skills transfer to real-world patients.
